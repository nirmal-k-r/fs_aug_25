BMW

BMW, standing for Bayerische Motoren Werke (Bavarian Motor Works), is a German multinational company founded in 1916 that manufactures luxury vehicles, motorcycles, and engines. The company initially produced airplane engines during World War I, later transitioning to motorcycles in the 1920s and automobiles in 1928. Headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, BMW is renowned for its innovative engineering, superior driving experience, and luxury vehicles, including its involvement with the Mini and Rolls-Royce brands within the larger BMW Group
